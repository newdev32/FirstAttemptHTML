The Origin of Ricotta Cheese

  • Ricotta is an Italian Whey Cheese made from Sheep, cow, goat or left over production of other cheeses from Italian Water Bufallo milk whey.
  • Ricotta literally meaning "recooked".
  • The production of ricotta in the Italian peninsula dates back to the Bronze Age.
